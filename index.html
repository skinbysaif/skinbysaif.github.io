About the clinic

A more personal kind of skin clinic.

Skin by Saif Clinic is a private Sheffield-based skin clinic offering advanced facials, microneedling, hydrafacial, dermaplaning and personalised skin consultations in a calm, carefully designed setting.

The clinic is built around thoughtful care, clean professional standards and treatments tailored to the person in front of you — not rushed, generic protocols. Whether your main concern is acne, congestion, dehydration, texture, pigmentation or overall skin confidence, every appointment is designed around real skin goals.

Meet your aesthetician

Built from lived experience, not just interest.

Skin by Saif began from something deeply personal. After struggling with severe cystic acne and feeling self-conscious in my own skin, I know first-hand how much skin concerns can affect confidence, comfort and the way you carry yourself day to day.

That experience is a big part of why this clinic exists. I wanted to create a space that feels warm, professional and genuinely understanding — where clients feel listened to, supported and guided toward realistic, long-term improvement rather than quick fixes.

My approach combines personalised treatment planning, careful consultation, strong hygiene standards and a real respect for how personal skin journeys can be.

FAQs

Everything you might want to know first.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

New clients can begin with a skin assessment so your concerns, routine and treatment suitability can be reviewed before building the most appropriate plan.

Are treatments personalised?

Yes. Treatments are adjusted around your skin type, concerns, sensitivity, current routine and treatment goals rather than using the same protocol for every client.

How is hygiene handled in clinic?

Cleanliness, careful preparation and professional working standards are a key part of the clinic experience, with a strong focus on safe, considered treatment practice throughout each appointment.

Can I book a course of treatments?

Yes. Courses may be booked at a discounted rate, and monthly payment options may be available depending on the treatment plan selected.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. NHS and Blue Light Card holders receive 15% off. New clients can also use SKIN10 for £10 off treatments over £50 or SKIN5 for £5 off treatments under £50.
