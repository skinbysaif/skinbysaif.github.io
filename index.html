About the clinic

A more personal kind of skin clinic.

Skin by Saif Clinic is built around the idea that great skin treatment should feel personal, considered and supportive from the very first appointment.

The clinic mission is to help clients feel more confident in their skin through honest guidance, tailored treatment planning and a calm, professional one-to-one experience.

Treatments are carefully selected to target common concerns like acne, congestion, dehydration, pigmentation, texture and overall skin rejuvenation, with every appointment adapted to the individual rather than a one-size-fits-all approach.

Meet your aesthetician

Built from lived experience, not just interest.

Skin by Saif began from something deeply personal. After struggling with severe cystic acne and feeling self-conscious in my own skin, I know first-hand how much skin concerns can affect confidence, comfort and the way you carry yourself day to day.

That experience is a big part of why this clinic exists. I wanted to create a space that feels warm, professional and genuinely understanding - where clients feel listened to, supported and guided toward realistic, long-term improvement rather than quick fixes.

My approach combines personalised treatment planning, careful consultation, strong hygiene standards and a real respect for how personal skin journeys can be.

FAQs

Everything you might want to know before booking.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

New clients can begin with a free pre-treatment consultation or a full skin assessment so your concerns, routine and treatment suitability can be reviewed before building the most appropriate plan.

What is the Ultimate Skin Rejuvenation Facial?

This is a premium combination treatment that brings together Hydrafacial and microneedling in one appointment to deeply cleanse, hydrate, refresh and support skin rejuvenation, with facial massage, red light therapy and a free pre-treatment consultation included.

Are treatments personalised?

Yes. Treatments are adjusted around your skin type, concerns, sensitivity, current routine and treatment goals rather than using the same protocol for every client.

How is hygiene handled in clinic?

Cleanliness, careful preparation and professional working standards are a key part of the clinic experience, with a strong focus on safe, considered treatment practice throughout each appointment.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

Some treatments may require consultation or suitability checks beforehand. New clients are encouraged to book a free pre-treatment consultation or skin assessment if they are unsure where to start.

Where in Sheffield are you based?

The clinic is based in Park Hill, Sheffield, in a convenient central location. Full arrival details can be provided after booking.

Is Park Hill easy to get to?

Yes. Park Hill is centrally located and easy to access from Sheffield city centre and surrounding areas, making it convenient for clients across Sheffield.

Can I book a course of treatments?

Yes. Courses may be booked at a discounted rate, and monthly payment options may be available depending on the treatment plan selected.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. NHS and Blue Light Card holders receive 15% off. New clients can also use SKIN10 for £10 off treatments over £50 or SKIN5 for £5 off treatments under £50.
